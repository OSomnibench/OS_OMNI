\section{Conclusion}

We presented \dataset, a cross-platform executable benchmark and environment for evaluating generalist computer-using agents across desktop, mobile, and web environments. Our experiments show that current agents remain far below human performance and are especially brittle on hard, cross-application, and cross-platform workflows, highlighting the need for stronger long-horizon planning ability for generalist CUA.

\paragraph{Limitation.}
\dataset covers five OS and diverse applications, but cannot include every software ecosystem or hardware-dependent workflow. Some platforms also require specific runtime resources, such as Apple hardware or activated Windows environments. Future versions can expand task coverage and add more controlled studies of observation modes and agent scaffolding.
